# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project harassment-free for everyone,
regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender
identity and expression, experience, education, socioeconomic status,
nationality, appearance, race, caste, color, religion, sexual identity and
orientation, or technical choices.

## Expected behavior

Be respectful, constructive, empathetic, and focused on the work. Accept
responsibility, apologize when appropriate, and prioritize community safety.
Harassment, insults, threats, sexualized attention, doxxing, and sustained
disruption are unacceptable.

Report incidents privately through the repository security contact. Maintainers
may edit or remove contributions and temporarily or permanently ban participants
whose behavior is inappropriate. This policy is adapted from Contributor
Covenant 2.1.
