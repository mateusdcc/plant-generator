# Academic references

Primary conceptual reference:

Prusinkiewicz, P., and Lindenmayer, A. _The Algorithmic Beauty of Plants_.
Springer-Verlag, 1990. Chapters 1–8 and Appendix A.

Implementation comments cite relevant chapters/sections without copying prose,
figures, photographs, or source listings. The package independently expresses
the algorithms in typed, bounded, renderer-neutral form. The book and its
illustrations are not distributed or licensed with this project.
